## 2. Identified Market Gaps (The "Blue Ocean")

### A. The "Messy-Doc" Ingestion Gap
Competitors struggle with unstructured "real world" data. Pro se litigants and small businesses don't have clean document sets; they have shoeboxes of receipts, screenshots of texts, and nested email threads.
*   **Competitor Status**: Basic OCR only.
*   **ChiefLitigator Solution**: Multi-modal ingestion that reconstructs timelines from disparate, messy sources.

### B. The "Last Mile" Filing Gap
Even the most advanced AI (Harvey/CoCounsel) stops at the "Download" button. The user still has to navigate complex, dated court portals (PACER, Tyler, etc.).
*   **Competitor Status**: No direct filing.
*   **ChiefLitigator Solution**: Headless browser agents that navigate portals and file documents autonomously.

### C. The "Certified Mail" Gap
Legal service often requires physical certified mail with return receipts. No major legal AI platform integrates this into the agentic workflow.
*   **Competitor Status**: Manual process.
*   **ChiefLitigator Solution**: Integrated API (e.g., Lob) for sending physical service of process via agents.
